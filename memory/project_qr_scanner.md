Building a free online QR scanner site for the Russian market. Goal: SEO traffic → ad revenue (AdSense/РСЯ).

**Why:** User wants passive income from a useful utility site. Competitor analysis shows top 2 Russian QR decoder sites can't even decode some QR codes — real competitive advantage.

**How to apply:** Focus on Russian SEO keywords, Yandex+Google optimization. Site is static HTML (no backend), planned for GitHub Pages hosting. Design style: Energetic (bold orange, thick borders, Limelight font). Competitor benchmark: code-qr.ru/decoder gets ~1000 visitors/day.
